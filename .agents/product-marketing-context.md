**What it does:** Provides end-to-end custom packaging manufacturing — from design and dieline creation to sampling, mass production, and logistics — specializing in child-resistant (CR) compliant packaging for regulated industries.

## Problems & Pain Points
**Core problem:** Cannabis and regulated product brands struggle to find packaging suppliers that combine compliance (CR certification), custom design capability, competitive pricing, and reliable delivery — especially when sourcing from overseas.
**Why alternatives fall short:**
- Local suppliers: High cost, limited customization options
- Generic overseas suppliers: Poor quality control, no compliance expertise, communication barriers
- DIY approach: Time-consuming, risk of non-compliance, design inconsistencies
**What it costs them:** Delayed product launches, compliance fines, lost shelf appeal, wasted budget on failed packaging runs.
**Emotional tension:** Fear of regulatory non-compliance, frustration with unreliable suppliers, anxiety about brand reputation if packaging fails.

## Competitive Landscape
**Direct:** Other Chinese packaging manufacturers (e.g., PackMojo, ePackaging) — fall short because they lack cannabis-specific compliance expertise and CR certification knowledge.
**Secondary:** Local US packaging distributors — fall short because of higher costs, longer lead times, and limited custom options.
**Indirect:** Generic packaging marketplaces (Alibaba, Made-in-China) — fall short because of quality inconsistency, no design support, and compliance risk.
